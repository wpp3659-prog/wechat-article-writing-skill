# Review Checklist

Use this after drafting and before final output.

## Topic And Reader

- The article directly addresses the user's topic.
- The intended reader is clear from the examples and wording.
- The article has one main argument or value promise.

## Title

- Title matches the body.
- Title is specific and not clickbait.
- Title is not too long for WeChat reading.
- Backup titles explore different angles, not tiny wording changes.

## Body

- Opening is not generic.
- H2 sections do not repeat the same point.
- Each section contains a concrete detail, scenario, method, or judgment.
- Paragraphs are phone-friendly.
- Lists are used only when they improve readability.
- No invented facts, data, quotes, or personal stories.

## Voice

- The article uses one clear expression style rather than generic public-account tone.
- The article has a human editorial voice.
- The prose avoids "AI assistant" tone.
- There is at least one sentence with a memorable point of view.
- The ending feels natural for the article type.

## AI-Trace Risk

- Sentence lengths vary naturally; no long run of similarly sized sentences.
- Paragraph lengths vary; no repeated section template.
- The next sentence is not always the most obvious continuation.
- Every 400-600 Chinese characters include at least one natural turn: scene, objection, correction, short interruption, or specific example.
- Concrete scenes or verifiable facts anchor each major section.
- The article does not sound neutral from beginning to end.
- Transitions are not dominated by common AI connectors.
- Personal-sounding claims are either user-provided, clearly hypothetical, or marked with editor notes.

## Word Count

- Exact user count: within plus or minus 10%.
- Range: inside the requested range.
- Default: normal article should be around 1800-2200 Chinese characters.

## Final Extras

- Summary is 80 Chinese characters or less.
- Golden lines are extracted from the article or lightly refined from it.
- Interaction prompt fits the topic.
- Quality report is concise and honest.
